Synopsis

A dreamlike novel, Hard Boiled Wonderland and the End of the World is split between parallel narratives. The odd-numbered chapters take place in the "Hard-Boiled Wonderland​". The narrator is a "Calcutec", a human data processor/encryption system who has been trained to use his subconscious as an encryption key.​ The even-numbered chapters deal with a newcomer to "The End of the World", a strange, isolated Town purportedly surrounded by a perfect and impenetrable wall. The narrator is in the process of being accepted into the Town​, but there is something eerily disquieting about its changeless nature and fable-like inhabitants. The two storylines converge, exploring concepts of consciousness, the unconscious mind, and identity.
